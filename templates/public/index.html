Real-Time Animal Detection

Our system uses advanced AI and machine learning techniques to process live CCTV footage and detect the presence of wild animals in real-time. By analyzing both video frames and audio inputs, the system identifies animal species accurately and quickly. This allows timely alerts to be sent to nearby residents and forest officials, reducing the risk of human-wildlife conflicts and ensuring community safety. The real-time processing capability ensures that any potential threats are identified as they occur, providing an efficient and practical solution for wildlife monitoring and safety.




GPS-Powered Alerts

Our system integrates GPS technology to enhance the precision of its alert mechanism. By utilizing GPS coordinates from the CCTV cameras, the system identifies the exact location of a detected animal. It then sends targeted alerts to residents and forest officers within a defined radius of the incident. This ensures safety measures are activated in the right areas, helping to minimize panic and improve response times. The GPS-powered alerts provide a reliable way to inform communities and authorities about potential wildlife threats in real time.
